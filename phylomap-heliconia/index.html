This application lets you explore a phylogenetic tree and associated species location data. Click on a node to expand/collapse its descendants recursively. Shift-click on a node corresponding to a clade to highlight all locations and species contained with the clade.

Modern species appear in the leaves of the tree, while the nodes represent common ancestors of the modern species that lived at various times in the past.
